1. Dashboard Overview

This is your dashboard where you'll find an overview of your sales, orders, account health, and more.

Practice: Try clicking on the different sections of the dashboard to get familiar with the layout.

2. Inventory Management

This is where you manage your products, add new items, update stock levels, and view your inventory.

Practice: Add a new product or update an existing product's inventory details.

3. Product Listings

Here you can create and manage your product listings on Amazon. Add titles, descriptions, and images for each product.

Practice: Try adding a new product and filling in product details.

4. Orders Management

This section allows you to view orders, track shipping, handle returns, and manage customer service issues.

Practice: Manage an incoming order and simulate processing a return.

5. Advertising & Reports

Create campaigns to promote your products, track ad performance, and view detailed reports about your sales and advertising efforts.

Practice: Try setting up an advertising campaign for one of your products.

6. Account Settings

Modify your account settings, including tax info, business details, and security preferences.

Practice: Update your account settings and security preferences.

7. Messages

Communicate with Amazon support, customers, and partners through the message center.

Practice: Send a test message to a customer or Amazon support.

Download a sample Excel sheet to manage your inventory and order details.

Practice: Download the Excel file, fill it with your product details, and update it as if you were managing a real store.
